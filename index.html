Adpact

What if you could give money to social purpose organizations by simply
going about your daily routines on platforms like Amazon, Facebook and Instagram.


Overview

What is
Adpact?

Adpact is a platform that gives the power of philanthropy to consumers to allocate money from major corporations to fund social purpose orgs. Adpact users decide which orgs to fund and they acrue donations based on their ad engagement.

Our Mission

We believe in the possibility of a healthy planet, a flourishing society and that it is the responsibility to of big business to give back to it's consumer base. The Adpact is an agreement to hold space for philanthropy within consumer transactions.

How is
Adpact Different?

Unlike Amazon Smile, Change.org and Facebook donate quick links, funding doesn't come out of the consumers pocket.


The vision

Users who sign up with the Adpact accrue funds based on their impressions and ad engagement on partner platforms like Facebook, Youtube, Instagram, Amazon and Spotify. Funds accrued in the user's adstash are passed onto a registered non-profit or public service agency. This incentivises the user to stay active and engaged with our partner platforms and adds value to their public image and boosts their brand equity.

Adpact lets users contribute to positive social change without using their own money. Imagine helping make the worl a better place with just a few clicks or swipes of your finger.



How it works

Clara is a 32 year old female and considered mid to low income.Clara logs onto Facebook to make a post about her child's first day of school and then spends about an hour checking out what her friends and family are posting.

Clara logs onto Amazon to do an hour of shopping and clicks on 5 ads, Amazon deposits 25 cents in her adstash.At the end of the month Clara logs into Adpact and checks her adstash balance which has accrued $7.95.

She has quick link buttons on her page to various social purpose orgs and the option of choosing how much she wants to donate to each. She decides to give $5 to Planned Parenthood and saves the rest until she accrues more money.

After donating Clara is offered suggestions for friends on Facebook who also donated through the Adpact and a quick link to the Adpact's Facebook group where she can connect with other users and their issues or advocate for her own.

Clara along with 20,000 other people donated an average of $5 a piece to Planned Parenthood for the month. Cumulatively, $100,000 was donated to Planned Parenthood.



Current Climate

In today's political climate many progressive issues are being challenged and the current administration is withdrawing funding from the EPA, cutting around 42% from programs that focus on clean water, clean air and climate change as well as roughly $100 million per year from Planned Parenthood. We need creative solutions for generating capital where there is none to fund the organizations that spread powerful ideas, do innovative research and create opportunities to care through action.

The Adpact takes social media platforms beyond connectivity and transforms them into tools for making the real world a better place.

Adpact is an authentic way to tap into consumers' need to feel they are contributing to the world.
